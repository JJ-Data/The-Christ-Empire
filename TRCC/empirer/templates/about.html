Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Raji Biodun Babatunde also known as PBB is the Lead pastor of The Risen Christ Christian Center, popularly known as Christ Empire - a ministry with a God-given mandate of fixing the world via the word of God and leading people to Heaven. He is also the General Coordinator of The Boanerges fellowship, an interdenominational mentoring group which is been committed to raising giants in the body of Christ for the past eighteen years. He is a seasoned teacher of the word, his messages are applicable to every aspect of life, they are relevant to every generation and life transforming. PBB is passionate about engaging young people, mentoring and helping Christians to grow into mature, competent and representative of Christ in every works of life God has placed them. He is trained as a Sports Coach with B.sc Business Administration in view from University of the people, USA. He is happily married to Raji-Babatunde Idowu and blessed with two children.
